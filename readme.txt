The AlphaPay Payments plugin allows you to accept Wechat Pay and Alipay via AlphaPay payment gatway on your WordPress site easily.

== Description ==

Allow Canadian merchants to easily accept Wechat Pay and Alipay for their E-commerce Platform with Alpha Pay Payment Gateway . Users pay in Chinese RMB and merchants receive Canadian dollars ($CAD). 

= Why choose Alpha Pay? =
Alpha Pay is a Canadian Payment Service Provider authorized by Alipay and WeChat Pay. 

Alpha Pay specializes in cross-border POS & mobile payment and marketing, dedicating to providing a safe and convenient RMB cross-border payment platform for Chinese consumers and Canadian companies. 

We make integrating Alipay and WeChat Pay for Canadian merchants easy and help enhance their share of 131 million Chinese consumer market.



More information is on [Alpha Pay Website](https://www.alphapay.ca) and [contact us](https://www.alphapay.ca/apply) to get your account ready.


== Features and Benefits ==
* One plugin integrates both Wechat Pay and Alipay. Merchants can enable Wechat Pay or Alipay or Both. 
* Support pay from scanning QR Code on PC.
* Accept showing QR Code in Alpha Pay page or Local page.
* Support native pay from wechat or Alipay Application on mobile (without extract QR Code).
* Accepts full and partial refunds.
* Customers pay with RMB and merchants receive CAD.
* Currency accepts CAD & CNY.
* Deposit to Canadian bank account directly.


== Installation ==
 
* In Wordpress -> Plugins menu, click “Add New”.  In the search field, search by “Alpha Pay - Wechat Pay & Alipay for WooCommerce”.
* Install and activate the plugin.
* Go to Alpha Pay' Website and [apply a merchant account](https://www.alphapay.ca/apply).
* After receive account information, you should enter Partner Code and Gateway Credential.
* Enable Wechat Pay and Alipay.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= The order showing on woocommerce is not paid, but the customer has already paid with Wechat Pay or Alipay. Where should I check the order status? =
1. Login the [Alpha Pay BackOffice](https://pay.alphapay.ca) with your Login ID and password.
2. In Woocommerce Order Details, find the alphapay_order_id and copy this number.
3. In Alpha Pay BackOffice, search the order_id you found in Woocommerce Order Details and check the order status.
4. If Pay Success in Alpha Pay BackOffice, change the order status in Woocommerce.

If you need more help, countact us 1-855-937-7888

 
= Does this require an SSL certificate? =

Yes! an SSL certificate must be installed on your site to use Alpha Pay (Wechat Pay and Alipay) which make sure payment complete under secure circumstance.
